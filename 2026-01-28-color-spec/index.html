Stratum is a visual identity system built on principles of clarity, depth, and precision. The green-forward palette conveys growth and innovation, while the typographic system ensures legibility across all applications. These guidelines establish the foundation for consistent brand expression.

Brand Personality

Confident but approachable

Stratum speaks with authority without being cold. We lead with clarity, not complexity. Our voice is direct, warm, and occasionally unexpected. We believe expertise should feel accessible, not intimidating.
