# Fix It PDX — data provenance

`reports.json` is generated by `_build-reports.py` (re-run: `python3 _build-reports.py`).

## Real data
**Pothole pins (372 of 664)** are a decimated, mixed-status sample of the City of
Portland's public **PBOT Maintenance — Pothole Repair Reports** feature layer:

- Layer: `https://www.portlandmaps.com/arcgis/rest/services/Public/PBOT_Maintenance/MapServer/0`
- This is the same data behind the city's [Potholes Reported & Repaired](https://www.arcgis.com/apps/webappviewer/index.html?id=1606bfad51a34a99a5d11d8016bd78ae) app.
- Fields used: `ITEM_STATUS` (Open / In Progress / Closed), `ITEM_DATE_CREATED`
  (epoch ms), `LOCATION_NEIGHBORHOOD`. Coordinates are WGS84.
- Status map: `Open → reported`, `In Progress → in_progress`, `Closed → fixed`.
- The layer has **no repair/close date**, so real "fixed" potholes carry **no
  `daysToFix`** — we never fabricate one. Their pin story shows "Repaired" without
  a fabricated duration.
- Pins marked `"real": true`.

## Synthetic data
**The other 11 categories (292 pins)** are illustrative placeholders until the
city's per-category feeds are wired in. They are:
- anchored to **real sampled pothole coordinates** (so they sit on real Portland
  streets), with small jitter;
- given realistic per-category status mixes and date spreads;
- given a `resolvedDate` / `daysToFix` only on `fixed` pins (these power the
  honest per-pin "fixed in X days" copy);
- marked `"real": false`.

## Stats
`stats.reportedThisMonth` / `stats.fixedThisMonth` are computed over the dataset
actually shown on the map (so the numbers reconcile with visible pins). "This
month" is June 2026 relative to the snapshot date. Reported-this-month is
dominated by real pothole reports; fixed-this-month counts only pins with a known
`resolvedDate` in the month (real closed potholes lack a date and aren't counted).

## Production path
Each category maps to a real Portland source (PBOT Maintenance layer, PedPDX,
Parks, the city's graffiti/obstruction/311 systems). Swapping a category from
synthetic to real = point the build script at that layer and normalize into the
`MapPin` schema in `../types.ts`. The UI needs no changes.

- IRP Campsite Reports feature service (schema reference only; campsite is out of
